About Kousei Arima

Kousei Arima is the main character from the anime "Your lie in April" which is one of the greatest and saddest anime known. Kousei is a pianist who was the youngest winner of "Maihou Piano Competition"
Kouse Arima is a teenage boy with short, black hair and blue eyes. He is of average height. He is a bit taller than Kaori Miyazono, who is a violinist who fell in love with Kousei, but sadly she dies in the end because of a serious illness she faced. They are both around the same is, between 14-15
